Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about astrology and our services

What is astrology and how does it work?

Astrology is the study of the movements and relative positions of celestial bodies interpreted as having an influence on human affairs. By analyzing your birth chart, astrologers provide guidance on personality, career, health, and relationships.

What details are required for a personal astrology reading?

We need your full name, date of birth, time of birth, and place of birth to create an accurate horoscope and provide personalized insights.

Can astrology help me make better career or business decisions?

Yes, astrology can help you understand your strengths and the most favorable time periods for starting new ventures, making investments, or changing careers.

Is astrology useful for marriage and relationship compatibility?

Absolutely. Kundali matching (horoscope matching) is an important part of marriage decisions in Vedic astrology. It assesses compatibility in areas like personality, emotional connection, and long-term harmony.

How accurate are astrological predictions?

Astrology is not magic, but when done by an experienced astrologer using correct birth data, it can be surprisingly accurate in identifying patterns and timing. It offers guidance, not guarantees.

Do you provide remedies or solutions for planetary issues?

Yes, we suggest effective Vedic remedies like mantras, gemstones, fasting, and rituals based on your horoscope to reduce the impact of malefic planets and improve overall well-being.
